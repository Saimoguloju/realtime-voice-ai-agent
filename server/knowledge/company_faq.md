# Nimbus Software — Support Knowledge Base

Nimbus is a fictional project-management SaaS used to demo this voice agent's
retrieval-augmented generation. Replace these documents with your own domain
content — product docs, clinic policies, internal wikis — and the agent will
answer from them.

## Plans and pricing

Nimbus offers three plans. The Free plan supports up to 3 projects and 5
teammates, with 1 GB of file storage. The Pro plan costs 12 dollars per user
per month and includes unlimited projects, 100 GB storage, timeline views, and
priority email support. The Enterprise plan has custom pricing and adds SSO
with SAML, audit logs, a dedicated success manager, and a 99.9 percent uptime
SLA. Annual billing gives a 20 percent discount on Pro and Enterprise.

## Free trial

Every new workspace starts with a 14-day Pro trial. No credit card is required
to start the trial. When the trial ends, the workspace automatically downgrades
to the Free plan; no data is deleted, but features beyond Free-plan limits
become read-only until you upgrade.

## Refunds and cancellation

You can cancel anytime from Settings, then Billing. Monthly subscriptions are
not refunded for the current month but will not renew. Annual subscriptions
canceled within 30 days of purchase receive a full refund; after 30 days the
refund is prorated for unused full months. Refunds are returned to the original
payment method within 5 to 10 business days.

## Password reset and login issues

To reset a password, click "Forgot password" on the sign-in page and follow the
email link, which expires after 60 minutes. If the email does not arrive, check
spam and confirm the address with your workspace admin. Accounts lock for 15
minutes after 10 failed sign-in attempts. Single sign-on users must reset their
password with their identity provider, not with Nimbus.

## Data export and migration

Workspace admins can export all projects as CSV or JSON from Settings, then
Data Export. Exports include tasks, comments, attachments metadata, and audit
history. File attachments are exported as a separate ZIP archive. Imports are
supported from Trello, Asana, and Jira through the built-in migration wizard.

## Support hours and contact

Standard support is available by email at support@nimbus.example and answers
within one business day. Pro customers get priority support with a 4-hour
first-response target during business hours, Monday through Friday, 9am to 6pm
US Eastern. Enterprise customers have a 24/7 hotline and a dedicated Slack
channel. Status and incident history are published at status.nimbus.example.

## Security and compliance

Nimbus is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted in
transit with TLS 1.3 and at rest with AES-256. Data is hosted in AWS us-east-1
by default; Enterprise customers can choose an EU region for data residency.
Two-factor authentication is available on all plans and can be enforced
workspace-wide by admins on Pro and above.
